Even though many of us aren't here for the cold weather, it's hard to deny that there's something so captivating about the falling snow. Sure, once February rolls around and the sky is grey, you probably want to scream at the sight of a mound of snow blocking in your car. In fact, I've found myself to be totally against winter more times than once. But, without fail, most of us seem to fall in love with the first snowfall of the season each year
